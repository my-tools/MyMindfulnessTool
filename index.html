Description

Incremental / timer game of increasing complexity and options

  • Earliest level may be "Count 10 breaths" with a countdown timer of 1 minute before next action can be performed
  • May be repeated indefinitely for achievements points
  • May upgrade to new techniques ("Perfect 10" - visualize fully the numbers 1 - 10 as you count. At the end, you give feedback when your attention begins to falter.)
  • Many possible techniques, topics to meditate on, duration, mantras, sounds.
  • Focus tasks require user to touch screen while focusing and let go when their focus falters (or reverse)
  • Charting of your progress
  • Increasing score with tongue-in-cheek titles to motivate user (Nirvana = -1 pt but your score increases from 0)
